# Design Context — Fenrir Ashenveil

## Design Context

### Users
Penggemar sci-fi, worldbuilding, dan komunitas roleplay yang ingin menjelajahi universe OC Fenrir Ashenveil. Mereka mencari pengalaman yang immersive, atmospheric, dan memberikan kedalaman narasi. Konteks penggunaan: browsing santai untuk menikmati visual dan cerita, atau referensi untuk roleplay/writing.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Mysterious, solitary, grounded
- **Tone**: Cinematic, documentary-style, rugged
- **3-word personality**: Isolated, resourceful, ancient
- **Emotional goals**: Evoke wonder, solitude, and the weight of a vast forgotten frontier

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Rugged utilitarian sci-fi, survivalist, grounded military-space ala *The Expanse* meets *Outer Wilds*
- **References**: Dune (cinematic desolation), Outer Wilds (cosmic mystery), The Expanse (grounded sci-fi), old military field manuals
- **Anti-references**: Neon cyberpunk, purple-blue AI gradients, glassmorphism, generic "gamer" aesthetics, cluttered dashboards
- **Theme**: Dark, earthy, ancient cosmic mystery with utilitarian military-sci-fi undertones

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep olive (`#3a3d2e`), earth brown (`#5c4a3a`), amber (`#c9a959`)
- **Background**: Warm dark (`#1a1814`), near-black with warm undertone (`#0f0e0c`)
- **Accents**: Rust/oxide orange (`#b85c38`), aged parchment (`#d4c5a9`)
- **Text**: Warm off-white (`#e8e0d0`), muted gold for highlights

### Design Principles
1. **Atmosphere over information**: Every element must reinforce the feeling of isolation and frontier exploration
2. **Utilitarian elegance**: Design should feel practical and worn, like equipment that has seen use
3. **Mystery through restraint**: Reveal information gradually; never explain everything at once
4. **Earthy darkness**: Avoid pure black/white; use warm, tinted darks that feel organic and aged
5. **Immersive navigation**: The interface itself should feel like an artifact — an archive dossier or field manual
